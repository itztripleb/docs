---
title: 'Refferals'
description: 'Satoshi perps referral program will offer users incentives to refer their friends!'
---

Traders who refer someone will receive tiered discounts and rewards based on the number of active referred users:

- **1-5 weekly active referrals:** 1.5% discount on trading fees + 1.5% of the referred users' trading fees.
- **5-15 weekly active referrals:** 2.5% discount on trading fees + 2.5% of the referred users' trading fees.
- **15+ weekly active referrals:** 5% discount on trading fees + 5% of the referred users' trading fees.

Referrers also receive a bonus multiplier on their airdrop based on the number of referred users.
